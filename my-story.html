My Story

A glimpse into my life, passions, and the experiences that shaped who I am

What I Enjoy Doing

My typical day is a mix of routine and spontaneity. I love starting my mornings with a good cup of coffee while planning the day ahead. Some days are filled with productive work and focused activities, while others are more laid-back, dedicated to hobbies and spending time with people I care about.

"Every moment is an opportunity to create a memory worth keeping."

My Interests

I've always believed that life is too short to stick to just one passion. My interests are diverse and constantly evolving, but they all share a common thread: they bring me joy and help me connect with others.

Exploring Coffees

Whether it's trying different brewing methods, visiting new coffee shops, or experimenting with beans from various regions, I genuinely enjoy exploring the world of coffee. Coffee is more than just a drink, each cup tells a different story, and I appreciate the small details that make every brew unique and meaningful.

Playing Badminton

Whether it's a casual game or a competitive match, I enjoy playing badminton for the energy and focus it demands. The fast pace of the game challenges my reflexes, strategy, and endurance, pushing me to constantly improve. It also gives me a chance to unwind from a hectic week.

Exploring New Places

Whether it's visiting a new place, finding a quiet cafe, or simply taking a different route than usual, I love exploring new places and experiencing unfamiliar environments. For me, exploring isn't just about going somewhere different, it's about growing through the experiences and stories I collect along the way.

Random Fun Facts

  • I used to spend 2 hours on the road when I was in high school.
  • My real dream job was a doctor, but unfortunately i am scared of blood.
  • Computer Science was never on my mind growing up, but here I am.
  • I really like a strong cappuccino on the evening to calm myself.
  • One of my goals is to visit different countries as much as possible before settling down.

Thanks for taking the time to learn about me. This is just a snapshot of who I am, there's always more to discover, and I'm excited to see where life takes me next!
